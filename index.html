During the Summer of 2018, the decay of a Higgs boson into a bottom quark-antiquark pair (H→bb̅) was finally observed. By combining over 30 years of experience with modern techniques, experts at the CMS experiment at CERN crafted an analysis of the VH(bb̅) decay channel which contributed significantly to this historic result.
